    Immerse yourself in a world of feathers and eggs! In our tournament, each chicken is unique and its fate is tied to the value of its egg. At the start, you will receive a card with your personalized chicken and an egg of random value (wood, bronze, silver, gold, emerald or diamond). Take on other players randomly in one-on-one duels! The player with the highest value egg will win. Advance through the elimination brackets: round of 16, quarterfinals, semifinals and final! Will you be the one to take the diamond egg and become the legend of the chicken coop? Don't miss it!
